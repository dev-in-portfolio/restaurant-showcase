Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need reservations for dinner or weekend brunch?

Reservations are strongly recommended for Friday & Saturday dinner service as well as Weekend Brunch. Walk-in seating is available at our walnut bar and lounge on a first-come, first-served basis. You can reserve online via our Reservations Page or call us at (704) 555-0198.

What are the parking options at 300 S Tryon St?

We offer valet parking directly at our main entrance on Tryon Street for $10. Self-parking is available in the covered 300 S Tryon parking garage, and metered street parking is available on surrounding blocks.

Is outdoor patio seating available?

Yes, our outdoor patio features covered seating with radiant heaters for year-round dining. Patio tables are seated on a first-come basis or by special reservation request.

Are private dining rooms available for corporate or group events?

Yes, our private dining room accommodates groups of up to 16 guests with custom wood-fired tasting menus. Please visit our Experience Page or contact us directly to inquire about date availability.
