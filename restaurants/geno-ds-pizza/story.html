Our Story

Three decades of recipe perfection brought to Charlotte's Uptown neighborhood.

Meet Geno D and Gena

A family passion for stretching dough and baking slices.

Geno has spent 35 years running hot-deck pizza ovens in standard pizzerias along the Jersey Shore. Over those decades, he refined the "Old World" family dough formulation — a recipe requiring long fermentation to yield a crust that is crispy on the outside, light on the inside, and strong enough to fold in half.

Alongside Gena, who manages ordering and front-of-house service, Geno set up his Charlotte kitchen in 2019. Together, they have created a warm neighborhood hub inside The Market at 7th Street, serving up classic slices with genuine family pride.

"Jersey Shore pizza is all about the crisp and the fold. If you can't walk down the boardwalk eating it, it isn't pizza."

— Geno D

The Journey

From boardwalk ovens to Queen City tables.

1980s – 2010s

Geno perfects his family sauce and dough recipes while baking in iconic Jersey Shore pizzerias, developing the thin square Grandma recipe.

2019

Geno D's opens its counter inside The Market at 7th Street, bringing authentic Jersey pies to Uptown Charlotte.

2021

Geno and Gena expand the menu to include house-made focaccia paninis and gluten-friendly cauliflower bases, becoming a local favorite.

Today

A staple of the Uptown lunch and dinner community, serving over 15 varieties of specialty pies daily.
