Design for the perfect bake

A great Jersey Shore style pizza is built on ratios. The balance between sauce moisture, cheese thickness, and dough strength determines whether the slice retains its signature boardwalk fold. Consider these aspects when customizing your pie.

Baking tips

  • Crust thickness: Choose square thin Grandma for a crispier, denser chew, or Large NY-Style for foldable slices.
  • Sauce weights: Pesto strips and ricotta bases have less water than traditional tomato sauce, allowing for a crisper crust.
  • Topping combinations: Sweet peppadews pair beautifully with pepperoni and hot honey to create sweet-heat bakes.
