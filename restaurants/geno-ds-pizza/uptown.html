The Market at 7th Street

An authentic urban food hall experience.

Geno D's Pizza is situated directly inside the historic Market at 7th Street, positioned at Brevard and 7th Street. Located steps from the Charlotte Hornets' Spectrum Center arena, our space gathers the city's finest independent food purveyors and artisans under one roof.

You can capture the aroma of freshly baked yeast dough and hot pepperoni the second you step into the marketplace. Pull up a stool at our market counter and grab a fresh slice right out of the brick deck oven.
