Welcome to Geno D's

Bringing the boardwalk slice straight to the Queen City.

You can smell Geno D's the second you enter The Market at 7th Street. Family-owned and operated, we bring Jersey Shore style pies straight to Charlotte. Every pie starts with our signature crispy dough, topped with premium tomatoes and whole-milk mozzarella. Poured and baked fast by Geno and his daughter Gena, it's the ultimate Uptown neighborhood pizzeria.

🍕

Grandma Pizza

Baked square-thin Sicilian style crust, fresh mozzarella cheese, parallel marinara strips, and a signature pesto finish.

🌶️

Hot Honey Pies

Pepperoni and sweet South African peppadew peppers drizzled with fiery, organic hot honey syrup.

🧄

Boardwalk Knots

Hand-tied garlic knots, pizza dough pockets, cheddar cheese stix, and house-filled sweet cannoli.

Sides & Salads

Meet the Pizza Makers

Geno D and Gena

Geno has spent over three decades throwing, tossing, and baking in high-volume pizzerias along the Jersey Shore. Now, alongside his daughter Gena, he brings that exact same high-energy hospitality and secret red sauce recipe to Charlotte's Uptown community.
