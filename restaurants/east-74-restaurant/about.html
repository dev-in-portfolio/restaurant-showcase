Our Story

Where Monroe Road Meets Home

East 74 Restaurant is a family-owned diner sitting right on Monroe Road — Route 74 — in the heart of Matthews, North Carolina.

Since opening our doors, we've been serving up honest, homestyle American cooking with a traditional Greek twist. Our family menu reflects the heritage that makes a neighborhood diner feel like home.

With a close-knit team that knows most of our regulars by name, East 74 isn't a big commercial operation. It's the kind of place where the server remembers how you like your eggs, and families stop in after church on Sunday.
