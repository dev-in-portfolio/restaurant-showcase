Taste with a clear progression

A tasting flight at Matthews Social House works best when each selection has a purpose. Begin with an approachable option, move toward fuller aroma or body, and save the most intense choice for later. That order gives every pour enough room to be noticed and makes it easier for a group to compare preferences without rushing through the experience.

A simple way to decide

  • Move from lighter or cleaner profiles toward stronger or sweeter selections.
  • Use water and simple pretzels/bites between tastes to reset the palate.
  • Compare aroma, body, finish, and personal preference rather than looking for one correct winner.

This page is organized around a social tasting plan that helps groups compare drinks and pace an evening together. It is meant to make the choice clearer, not to replace a conversation with the restaurant. Specific dishes, ingredients, prices, hours, availability, service options, and policies can change. Confirm time-sensitive details through the restaurant’s current official channel, especially for allergies, large groups, events, or a visit built around one particular item.
