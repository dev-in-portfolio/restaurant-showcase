Comfort Cooking With Heart

A Charlotte landmark since 2012, serving authentic Southern soul food classics to East Independence visitors.

Family Kitchen Traditions

At Queen's, our menu highlights Southern staples made from scratch daily. We use premium pork, slow-simmered beef oxtails, and fresh greens seasoned using traditional techniques.

We avoid short-cuts, slow cooking our stocks and pan gravy to extract the deepest flavors. This careful, slow-cooking philosophy makes every plate feel like home.

Our Charlotte Landmark

Since opening on East Independence Boulevard, we have been proud to serve families, workers, and travelers coming through the Matthews and East Charlotte areas.

We offer a clean, family-friendly dine-in booth space and a fast takeout counter for those picking up dinner pans on their commute home.
