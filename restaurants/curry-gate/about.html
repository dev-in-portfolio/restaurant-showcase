Our Story

About Curry Gate

Bringing award-winning Indian and Nepali flavors to the Charlotte community.

Curry Gate was born from a simple vision: to share the rich, diverse flavors of Indian and Nepali cuisine with the Charlotte community. What began as a single restaurant serving family recipes has grown into five locations across the metro area, each rooted in the same commitment to authentic spices and fresh ingredients.

Authentic Spices

We source our raw spices directly, ensuring every single curry pot has the depth, aroma, and complexity of traditional Himalayan and sub-continental cooking.

Fresh & Hand-Made

Our flatbread naan is baked fresh inside our clay tandoor ovens, our momos are hand-folded daily, and all curry bases are slow-simmered in small batches.

Community Focus

We believe great food brings neighbors together. By expanding our taproom pairings and neighborhood presence, we make authentic dining more accessible.
