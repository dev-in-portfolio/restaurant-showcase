Taste with a clear progression

A tasting flight at Fenian's Keep works best when each selection has a purpose. Begin with an approachable option, move toward fuller aroma or body, and save the most intense choice for later. That order gives every pour enough room to be noticed and makes it easier for a group to compare preferences without rushing through the experience.

A simple way to decide

  • Move from lighter or cleaner profiles toward stronger or sweeter selections.
  • Use water and food between tastes to reset the palate.
  • Compare aroma, body, finish, and personal preference rather than looking for one correct winner.
