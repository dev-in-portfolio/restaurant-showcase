Diner Heritage

Our Story

Three generations of fresh-ground chuck patties in Monroe, NC.

Duke's Grill has been a Monroe institution since 1951, serving fresh-ground burgers to Union County families.

What started as a simple diner burger stand grew into a beloved local landmark where neighbors gather over hand-patted burgers, giant crispy onion rings, and sweet tea.

We are famous for our Slaw Burger — a signature creation voted the best burger in North Carolina by a statewide poll.

1951

Duke's Grill opens on Concord Avenue in Monroe.

1960s

The Slaw Burger is born — and becomes a local legend.

2019

Voted the best burger in North Carolina in a statewide poll.
