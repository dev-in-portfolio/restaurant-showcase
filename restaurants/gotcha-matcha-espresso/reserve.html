Walk-In Only Cafe

Gotcha Matcha is walk-in only. No reservations needed. Just come on in and our baristas will serve you fresh lattes!

Best Times to Visit

Early morning (7–9 AM)

Beat the rush. Grab your matcha and settle in before the office crowd arrives.

Mid-morning (9–11 AM)

Steady flow but manageable. Great for a productive work session.

Lunch hour (12–1 PM)

Peak time — office workers flood in. Expect a line, but it moves fast.

Afternoon (2–5 PM)

Chill vibes. Perfect for a pick-me-up matcha or espresso. Outdoor seating shines here.

Your First Visit

Walk in and look for the panda

Staff wear panda heads. The branding is bold and playful — you'll know you're in the right place.

Start with the signature

The Gotcha Matcha Latte ($6.75) is the perfect intro. Smooth, ceremonial grade, no bitterness.

Ask about flavors

Not sure about pandan or ube? Ask your barista — they'll walk you through it.

Try a pastry

The Ube Donut ($4.00) or Pandan Cinnamon Roll ($8.58) pair perfectly with any matcha.

Customization Guide

Every drink is customizable. Here's how to make it yours:

Sweetness Level

  • Unsweetened — Pure matcha flavor, no added sugar
  • Less Sweet — A hint of sweetness
  • Regular — Our standard balance
  • Extra Sweet — For those with a sweet tooth

Ice Level

  • No Ice — All drink, no dilution
  • Less Ice — More flavor, still cold
  • Regular Ice — Standard serving
  • Extra Ice — Iced and refreshed

Milk Choice

  • Oat Milk — Creamy, our default
  • Almond Milk — Light, nutty
  • Coconut Milk — Tropical twist
  • Dairy — Classic option
