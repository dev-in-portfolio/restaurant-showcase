Customizing the Perfect Sip

Our customizer allows you to mix and match flavor structures. To preserve the flavor balance of authentic stone-ground Japanese green teas, we recommend pairing our roasted Hojicha base with ube syrup, and Uji green matcha with aromatic pandan syrup.

Tips from the Baristas

  • Sweetness adjustment: All custom lattes include a default sweetness recipe. You can ask for half-sweetness if you prefer a stronger tea profile.
  • Milk options: We highly recommend oat milk as a base for matcha lattes to yield a thick, velvet texture that holds syrup spirals well.
  • Waffle Crumbs: The waffle cone crumbs on the glass lip provide a caramelized crunch with every sip.
