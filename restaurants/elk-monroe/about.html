Tavern Chronicles

Our Story

A neighborhood landmark serving scratch-made Southern favorites in historic downtown Monroe, NC.

Revitalizing Downtown Monroe

E.L.K. Tavern opened inside a beautifully restored brick building on the corner of East Franklin Street in historic downtown Monroe, NC.

Combining rustic brick walls with modern steel finishes, our space is designed for relaxing pub dining. Whether you sit at our custom timber bar or tuck into a cozy dining room booth, E.L.K. welcomes you to eat, drink, and socialize.

What is E.L.K.?

While our name inspires our mascot, E.L.K. stands for **Eggs, Legs, and Kegs**, representing our primary menu anchors. It also stands for our ultimate hospitality goal: **Eat Like a King**.

Core Anchors

• Eggs — deviled & scotch eggs
• Legs — double-fried crispy wings
• Kegs — NC craft drafts & spirits
• King — hospitable service

The Scratch Standard

Custom Ground Patties

Every single burger is pressed and griddled fresh daily using our custom beef blend.

House Scotch Eggs

Soft-boiled eggs hand-wrapped in pork sausage and rolled in crispy seasoned panko crumbs.

Local Micro-Brews

We partner with Union County and Charlotte regional craft breweries to source fresh seasonal kegs.
