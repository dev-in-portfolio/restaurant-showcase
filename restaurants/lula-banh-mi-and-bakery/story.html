Behind the Counter

Our Story

Bringing authentic Vietnamese flavors and scratch-made bakery to Pineville.

Lula Bánh Mì + Bakery was born from a simple idea: bring the vibrant, layered flavors of Vietnamese street food to Pineville — and pair them with the kind of scratch-made bakery that makes every visit feel special.

What started as a takeout-focused concept quickly became a neighborhood destination. Guests come for the grilled pork bánh mì with its crackling fresh baguette, stay for the vermicelli bowls piled high with herbs and egg rolls, and come back for the pandan coconut cake that tastes like it came from a fine patisserie.

Every baguette is baked in-house. Every macaron is piped by hand. Every boba drink is made to order with your choice of sweetness and toppings. It's the kind of care that turns a quick lunch into a daily ritual.

In 2022, Lula opened its doors at 10400 Centrum Parkway in Pineville. The space is bright and welcoming — designed for takeout, yes, but also for lingering over a Vietnamese coffee and a box of macarons. A second location followed in the Arboretum (Charlotte), bringing pho and expanded options to a wider community.

Whether you're stopping in for a crispy pork belly bánh mì, picking up a tray of assorted macarons for a celebration, or ordering family-sized chicken curry for dinner at home — Lula is here to make your day a little sweeter, a little spicier, and a whole lot more delicious.
