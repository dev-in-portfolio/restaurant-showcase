Our Sweet Story

Handcrafting premium ice cream and celebrating sweet moments in Pineville since opening our parlor doors.

Velvety Cream Foundations

At Carolina Scoops, we believe that premium ice cream begins at the source. That is why we partner with local Carolina dairy farmers who raise grass-fed cattle. Our cream base is delivered fresh to our kitchen, pasteurized gently, and mixed with premium raw organic cane sugar. We never use high-fructose corn syrup, artificial stabilizers, or pre-made liquid flavor mixes.

Handmade Small-Batch Process

Every single tray of ice cream on our menu board is churned in our Pineville kitchen using small-batch Italian batch freezers. Churning in small volumes limits the amount of air whipped into the cream, producing a dense, velvety consistency that melts slowly and delivers maximum flavor intensity on every spoon.

A Gathering Place for Celebrations

Ice cream has always been a symbol of celebration. From school assemblies and youth sports leagues to birthday parties and weddings, our goal is to bring neighbors together. Our outdoor patio space is designed to be a welcoming neighborhood retreat where you can slow down and enjoy a custom flavor flight paddle with family.
