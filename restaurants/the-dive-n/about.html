Serving Pineville Since 1978

A classic Southern drive-in diner kitchen focused on honest pricing, fast service, and griddled comfort food.

Flat-Top Diner Traditions

Our kitchen values simple, fast American diner staples. We press our burgers by hand, sear our melts on buttered rye, and bake biscuits from scratch every morning.

We operate a walk-in counter-style diner room on Polk Street, making it easy for Pineville locals, railroad crews, and travelers to grab a fresh hot bite.

Community Diner Space

The Dive N represents a slice of historic Pineville dining culture. We keep our prices transparent, our tables clean, and our coffee cups topped off.

While we do not support dine-in booth reservations, we welcome families, groups, and solo diners for first-come, first-served diner seating.
