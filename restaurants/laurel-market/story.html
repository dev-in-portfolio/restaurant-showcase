Our Story

A Charlotte institution rooted in the Myers Park neighborhood.

A Family-Owned Tradition

Laurel Market began as a small corner store in Charlotte's tree-lined Myers Park neighborhood. What started as a place to pick up milk and bread gradually became so much more — a gathering place, a lunch destination, and a beloved community landmark.

For generations, the same family has run the market with a simple philosophy: treat every customer like a neighbor, stock the best products you can, and make sandwiches that people will remember.

Over the years, the deli counter grew from a small cooler of pre-made sandwiches to a full-service operation where everything is made to order. Our Chicken Torta, The Traveler, and Special #9 have developed near-legendary status among Charlotte locals.
