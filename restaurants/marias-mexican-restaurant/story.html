Behind the Kitchen

Our Story

Bringing the bold, authentic flavors of Mexico to South Boulevard for nearly two decades.

Maria's Mexican Restaurant opened its doors on South Boulevard in Charlotte in 2008 with a simple mission: serve authentic Mexican food made with fresh ingredients, family recipes, and genuine hospitality.

What started as a neighborhood taqueria quickly became a South Charlotte destination. Word spread about the birria tacos — slow-braised beef simmered for hours in a rich, aromatic consume, then crisped on the griddle and served with melted cheese. Soon, people were driving across the city for Maria's birria.

In 2025, Maria's introduced Birria Ramen, a bold fusion that combined the deep, savory birria broth with ramen noodles. It was an instant hit — a dish that represents everything Maria's stands for: honoring tradition while embracing creativity.

Over the years, Maria's has grown from a small storefront into a beloved Charlotte institution. The dining room is warm and inviting, filled with the sounds of sizzling fajitas, clinking margarita glasses, and happy conversations. Whether you're stopping in for a quick lunch special, celebrating with family, or picking up a tray of birria to go — Maria's treats you like family.

Today, Maria's continues to serve the bold, authentic flavors that made it famous. From our signature birria to fajitas, enchiladas, and hand-shaken margaritas, every plate is made with love and spice. ¡Make it Maria's!
