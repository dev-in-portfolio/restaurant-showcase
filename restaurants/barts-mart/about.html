Sourcing & Arcade Roots

Our journey of bringing craft single cans, rotating local drafts, and retro cabinets to Charlotte's Eastway Crossing.

The Bottle Shop

At Bart's Mart, we are dedicated to celebrating the craft brewing community. Our shop contains a curated selection of over 250 craft single cans and natural wines in our walk-in coolers. Guests can create a customized 4-pack or enjoy a drink at our tables, creating a welcoming social space for neighbors.

Arcade Lounge & Classics

We believe in keeping retro gaming culture alive. Our lounge features physical arcade cabinets like Ms. Pac-Man and Street Fighter II, alongside classic pinball and consoles. Whether you are aiming for a high score or playing casually with friends, our cabinets bring classic arcade energy back to Charlotte.

Eastway Crossing Hangout

Located inside the Eastway Crossing shopping center on Eastway Dr, Bart's Mart offers a spacious patio, regular game tournaments, and a draft taproom. We serve as a community hub where local artists, gamers, and beer enthusiasts gather to enjoy quality drinks and classic games.
