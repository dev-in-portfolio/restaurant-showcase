Tastes of Ancient Anatolia in University City

A Turkish & Mediterranean cafe serving authentic kebabs, in-house pastries, Turkish coffee, and Moroccan herbal teas — crafted by Chef Omer Aydin with 15 years of Istanbul experience.

Explore the Menu Plan Your Visit

In-House Bakery

Borek, simit, baklava, and kunefe made from scratch daily. Our pastry case is the heart of the cafe — every item crafted in-house using time-honored Anatolian recipes.

Turkish Coffee & Tea

Traditional Turkish coffee brewed to perfection, specialty lattes, and a curated selection of Moroccan herbal teas — a nod to the Tarhan family's heritage.

Authentic Kebabs

Adana, chicken and beef shish, kofte, and mixed platters grilled to order. Every kebab is seasoned and prepared with the techniques Chef Omer honed in Istanbul.
