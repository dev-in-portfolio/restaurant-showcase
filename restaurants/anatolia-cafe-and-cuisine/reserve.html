Walk-Ins Welcome

No reservations needed — Anatolia is a casual, welcoming space where you can sit down and enjoy a meal at your own pace. Whether you're stopping by for a quick Turkish coffee and simit, or settling in for a full kebab platter with friends, we're ready for you.

Our staff will guide you through the menu, and the pastry case is always worth a look — you might discover a kunefe or pistachio cheesecake you didn't know you needed.

The Anatolia Experience

What to Expect

Authentic Turkish Pastries

From the pastry case to your table — borek, simit, baklava, kunefe, and eclairs baked fresh daily by Chef Omer.

Flame-Grilled Kebabs

Adana, shish, kofte, and mixed platters — every kebab is seasoned and grilled the way it would be in Istanbul.

Turkish Coffee & Tea

Traditional ibrik-brewed coffee, specialty lattes, and Moroccan herbal teas — a drink for every mood.

Perfect settings

Best For

Breakfast

The Turkish Breakfast Plate, Menemen, or a simit sandwich — paired with Turkish coffee or a Moroccan herbal tea. A perfect way to start the morning.

Casual Lunch

A borek and lentil soup, a quick kebab wrap, or a doner — fast, fresh, and satisfying. Great for a University City lunch break.

Family Dinner

The Mixed Kebab Platter, Ali Nazik, or a spread of flatbreads and appetizers — bring the family and share a feast Anatolian-style.

Sweet Treats

Pistachio baklava, kunefe, Dubai chocolate cake, or a slice of pistachio cheesecake — the pastry case is calling your name.
