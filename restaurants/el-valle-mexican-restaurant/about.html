About El Valle

A family dream rooted in the culinary traditions of a small Mexican village.

Our Name, Our Roots

El Valle — meaning "The Valley" — is named after the village in Jalisco, Mexico, where our founder's family roots run deep.

What started as a single small diner location in Matthews, NC was born from a simple idea: share authentic, homestyle Mexican food with a community far from home. Our founder brought recipes passed down for generations and opened the doors of El Valle for the first time in 1998.

From that original site, we have grown to serve Matthews and South Charlotte with warm hospitality, fresh-pressed tortillas, and local micro-brews.
