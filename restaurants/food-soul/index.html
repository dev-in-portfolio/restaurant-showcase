Home-Cooked Soul Food Tradition

At Food For Your Soul CLT, we capture the warmth of a Sunday dinner every day of the week. Located inside the City Kitch kitchen facility on Thrift Road, we prepare loaded plates filled with traditional recipes—slow-cooked green beans, golden croquettes, and scratch-baked cakes.
