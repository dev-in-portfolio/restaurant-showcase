Honest Comfort Gastronomy

A Charlotte Southern food destination situated in the historic Thrift Road district since 2012.

Family Kitchen Traditions

At Food For Your Soul, we believe comfort food is a craft. We slow-simmer our collards and green beans using smoked meats, hand-bread our chicken strips, and bake cakes using traditional, authentic recipes.

Our kitchen values rich seasonings, slow-cooking processes, and large, home-style portions that ensure no visitor leaves hungry.

City Kitch Operations

Operating out of the professional City Kitch commercial facility on Thrift Road, we focus on high-volume takeout, griddle pickups, and catering.

This specialized kitchen layout allows us to execute large party pans, group meeting platters, and custom cornbread bowls with maximum quality control.
