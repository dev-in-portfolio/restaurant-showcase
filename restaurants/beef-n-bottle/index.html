An Honest Charlotte Legacy

Welcome to Beef 'N Bottle. For over 65 years, our dining room has welcomed Charlotteans with its signature dark wood walls, red leather booths, and celebrity headshots. We focus on premium beef cuts grilled to absolute precision, classic sides, and a tavern pour that keeps the history of Charlotte's culinary scene alive.
