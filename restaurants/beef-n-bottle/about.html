Steakhouse Heritage

Beef 'N Bottle stands as one of Charlotte's oldest and most iconic culinary landmarks. Established in 1958, we have spent over six decades serving premium choice cuts of beef, fresh seafood, and vintage highballs. Our dining room preserves the classic speakeasy atmosphere that has welcomed generations of local diners.

Red Leather Booths

We believe in preserving the honest, dim lighting and rich mahogany walls that define our legacy. As guests dine under vintage celebrity headshots, they enjoy the same dedicated service and attention to detail that our founder envisioned in 1958.

Premium Aged Cuts

Every cut of beef served in our dining room is aged to perfection, hand-cut, and flame-grilled to absolute precision. Paired with classic steakhouse sides like creamed spinach and garlic mashed potatoes, we deliver a dining experience that honors our historic roots.
