Charlotte Traditions

Plaza Midwood Story

Serving classic Southern diner plates and local community hospitality since 2002.

For decades, Dish has stood as a welcoming culinary landmark in Charlotte's historic Plaza Midwood neighborhood, serving up scratch-made Southern favorites with a relaxed, friendly diner attitude.

Our recipes are rooted in authentic home cooking traditions — hand-rolling our famous flour dumplings, preparing our signature meatloaf glaze, and simmering savory collard greens every morning. We believe that good comfort food should be simple, generous, and served with a warm smile.

Whether you're stopping by for a comforting weekday lunch, enjoying a custom comfort plate, or joining us for weekend brunch, you're always part of the family at Dish.
