Southern Style Group Catering

Feed your church gathering, family picnic, or office team lunch with Monroe's favorite crispy fried chicken and fresh buttermilk biscuits.

Picnic & Family Packs

Planning a family get-together or park picnic in Monroe? We prepare 12-piece and 20-piece family packs featuring mixed crispy fried chicken, large side orders of macaroni & cheese or green beans, and flaky warm biscuits.

Church Potlucks

A Union County tradition. We specialize in bulk orders of 50, 100, or 200 pieces of hand-breaded fried chicken and boxes of fresh biscuits, packaged in hot containers for local church potlucks, assemblies, and reunions.

Office Tender Trays

Elevate office lunch. We package large trays of our seasoned crispy white meat chicken tenders, paired with massive portions of seasoned tater wedges, honey mustard dipping cups, and buttermilk biscuits.
