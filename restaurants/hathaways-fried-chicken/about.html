Serving Union County Since 1982

Monroe's favorite roadside chicken stop for over forty years.

A Roosevelt Blvd Landmark

Hathaway's Chicken n' Biscuits opened its doors in Monroe in 1982. Positioned on West Roosevelt Boulevard (Highway 74), the restaurant was built to feed highway travelers, local farmers, and Monroe families who wanted high-quality, hot Southern fried chicken served fast through our convenient drive-thru window or cozy lobby counter.

Over the decades, we have remained an independent, locally-operated country kitchen. While national fast-food chicken chains have opened nearby, Monroe residents continue to choose Hathaway's for our authentic Southern taste.

The Secret to the Crunch

At Hathaway's, we don't rush the frying process. We hand-bread our fresh chicken daily in our proprietary blend of Southern spices. We use specialized pressure-fryers that seal in the juices, ensuring that the meat stays incredibly tender and juicy on the inside while creating a thick, golden, crispy crust on the outside.

Our buttermilk biscuits are rolled by hand and baked fresh in our ovens throughout the day. If you order a "Business Man" dinner or a picnic box, you can count on your biscuit being warm and ready for butter.

Southern Specialties

We are proud to serve traditional Southern favorites that are hard to find elsewhere. Our fried chicken gizzards and chicken livers are prepared daily with the same care as our fried chicken, breaded and fried to a perfect golden crisp. It is a true taste of old-school North Carolina country cooking, served hot with a warm biscuit.
