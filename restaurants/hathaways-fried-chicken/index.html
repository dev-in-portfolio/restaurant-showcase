Crispy Chicken & Flaky Biscuits

"Made the traditional Southern way. Hand-breaded chicken, hot potato wedges, and flaky, golden buttermilk biscuits baked fresh throughout the day in Monroe."

A Union County Tradition

Located on Roosevelt Blvd (Highway 74) in Monroe, Hathaway's Fried Chicken has been serving Union County residents for generations. Our chicken is hand-breaded, perfectly seasoned, and pressure-fried to lock in the flavor. Every meal is served with our signature giant tater wedges and warm country buttermilk biscuits.
