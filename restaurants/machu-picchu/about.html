Culinary Spirit of the Andes

A dedicated, family-operated dining room serving Pineville with authentic Peruvian recipes since 2012.

Incan Culinary Traditions

At Machu Picchu, we honor the incredible richness of Peruvian gastronomy—often cited as one of the culinary capitals of the world. We build our recipes around key ingredients imported directly from South America, including Rocoto peppers, yellow Aji Amarillo, purple corn (Maíz Morado), and choclo.

From curing seafood in freshly squeezed lime juice to firing beef tenderloin in a red-hot wok, our cooking techniques guarantee authentic flavors.

Dine-In & Pisco Bar

Our main dining room on Main Street in Pineville features comfortable booth seating, a full-service bar highlighting Pisco sours, and a welcoming family atmosphere.

We support dine-in table settings, quick counter takeout, and complete party platter boxes for local events and corporate catering.
