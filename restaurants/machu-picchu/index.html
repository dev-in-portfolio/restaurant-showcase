The Spirit of the Andes

Machu Picchu Peruvian Cuisine brings the authentic tastes of Lima, Cusco, and the Pacific coast to Pineville. Since 2012, our kitchen has highlighted Peru's diverse culinary influences—from traditional stir-fry Lomo Saltado woks to modern seafood specialties dressed in Aji Amarillo and Rocoto pepper.
