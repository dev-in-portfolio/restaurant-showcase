Legacy & Story

A walk-up Southern food tradition on the corner of Brevard and 30th Streets since 1973.

Fifty Years in NoDa

Founded by CT Brooks Sr. in 1973, Brooks' Sandwich House began as a simple walk-up food counter serving nearby textile mills and neighborhood regulars. Decades before South Boulevard and North Davidson evolved into Charlotte's primary entertainment corridors, the red wooden stand was a local gathering spot.

Southern Diner Roots

Our approach to food has never changed. We have no indoor dining area and no table service. Guests order from our walk-up windows, receive their burgers wrapped in classic paper, and eat on the run or around our outdoor stand barrels. It is a traditional Southern food experience that honors Charlotte's working heritage.

Signature Chili Recipe

The cornerstone of our menu is our signature homemade beef chili. Prepared fresh daily using the same secret family recipe since 1973, it is cooked low and slow to top our burgers, hot dogs, and fries. Ordered "All The Way" with mustard and onions, it represents the definitive flavor of Charlotte dining history.
