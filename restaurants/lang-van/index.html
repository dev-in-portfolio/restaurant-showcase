Charlotte's Vietnamese Landmark

Authentic Vietnamese Culinary Tradition

Serving traditional phở, vermicelli bowls, summer salad rolls, and a complete vegetarian selection for over two decades in Charlotte.

A Charlotte Institution

For over 20 years, Lang Van has been beloved by locals for its warm hospitality and massive, flavor-packed menu. Run by Dan Nguyen and her family, the kitchen produces fresh, aromatic dishes using traditional Vietnamese herbs, long-simmered phở broths, and crispy rolls. Every guest is welcomed like family.

Popular Dishes

Famous Soup
Phở Đặc Biệt (House Special) $13.95

Aromatic beef bone marrow broth, simmered for 24 hours, filled with thin rice noodles, sliced steak, brisket, meatballs, and beef tripe. Served with fresh bean sprouts, Thai basil, lime, and jalapeños.

Noodle Bowl
Bún Thịt Nướng (Grilled Pork) $12.50

Cold rice vermicelli noodles topped with lemongrass-marinated grilled pork, shredded lettuce, fresh herbs, pickled daikon/carrots, crushed peanuts, and crispy shallots. Served with house nuoc cham sauce.
