The Culinary Anthology

With over 100 choices, our menu celebrates the diversity of traditional Vietnamese cuisine. Here are some of our most beloved guest selections.

Appetizers (Khai Vị)

Gỏi Cuốn (Summer Rolls) $8.50

Fresh cold rice paper rolls filled with shrimp, pork, fresh mint, bean sprouts, and vermicelli. Served with a rich house-made peanut dipping sauce.

Chả Giò (Crispy Spring Rolls) $7.95

Fried rice paper rolls stuffed with seasoned ground pork, carrots, glass noodles, and mushrooms. Served warm with a side of sweet chili fish sauce (Nước chấm).

Tofu Summer RollsV $8.00

Fresh rice paper wrap, crispy tofu sticks, fresh herbs, vermicelli, cucumber. Served with peanut sauce.

Traditional Phở

Phở Đặc Biệt (Beef Combo) $16.50

Aromatic beef bone broth simmered for 12 hours with star anise and cinnamon, served with rice noodles, sliced ribeye, flank steak, brisket, meatballs, and fresh tripe.

Phở Gà (Chicken Pho) $15.00

Light and clear chicken broth served with shredded organic breast meat, thin rice noodles, fresh herbs, scallions, and coriander leaves.

Phở Chay (Vegetarian Pho)V $15.00

100% plant-based vegetable broth, organic firm tofu blocks, sliced baby bok choy, carrots, mushrooms, and broccoli.

House Specialties

Bánh Xèo (Sizzling Pancake) $18.95

A crispy savory rice flour crepe colored with turmeric, filled with shrimp, pork slices, onions, and bean sprouts. Served with lettuce and herbs to wrap, and fish sauce dip.

Cơm Tay Cầm (Clay Pot Rice) $19.50

Steaming jasmine rice cooked in a traditional clay pot with ginger, mushrooms, chicken, and Chinese sausage, creating a delicious crispy rice crust at the bottom.

Bún Thịt Nướng (Vermicelli Bowl) $15.95

Cold vermicelli rice noodles topped with grilled lemongrass pork, chopped crispy spring roll, fresh mint, peanuts, pickled carrots, cucumber, and savory sauce.

Vegetarian Delights (Chay)

Đậu Hũ Xả Ớt (Lemongrass Tofu)V $16.00

Crispy fried organic tofu stir-fried with fragrant minced fresh lemongrass, bell peppers, onions, chili flakes, and soy glaze. Served with steamed jasmine rice.

Cà Ri Chay (Vegetarian Curry)V $16.50

A rich, aromatic yellow coconut milk curry filled with sweet potatoes, carrots, tofu blocks, bamboo shoots, and green beans. Served with rice or warm bread.

Rau Muống Xào Tỏi (Garlic Water Spinach)V $14.50

Fresh water spinach stems and leaves flash-sautéed in a hot wok with generous crushed garlic, white pepper, and light soy sauce.
