Family Platters & Large Groups

Let Lang Van provide comforting, authentic Vietnamese meals for your next family gathering or team lunch.

Group Take-Out Orders

Need to feed a group? We specialize in large take-out orders for businesses and family events. We package our vermicelli bowls, summer rolls, fried spring rolls, and signature entrees to travel perfectly. Please call us at least 2 hours in advance.

Dine-In Family Banquets

Book a large table block for groups of 6 to 12 guests. Dan and the staff will work with you to prepare a family-style dining banquet, featuring multiple shared clay pots, sizzling pancakes, and steaming bowls of beef and chicken pho.

Dietary Curation

Hosting an event with diverse dietary needs? Our vegetarian and vegan selection is legendary. We can structure custom group platters that are entirely dairy-free, gluten-free, or vegan without compromising on traditional flavors.
