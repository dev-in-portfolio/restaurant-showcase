Our Story

Charlotte's beloved Vietnamese kitchen serving the community since 2002.

A Family Legacy

Founded in 2002 on Shamrock Drive by Dan Nguyen, Lang Van was built on a simple premise: serving authentic, comforting Vietnamese meals cooked from the heart. Dan's incredible memory—remembering regular customers' orders even after years of absence—became Charlotte legend, drawing food lovers from all over the metro area.

While Charlotte has grown rapidly around us, our independent family restaurant has remained a constant. We are proud to be a gathering place where families share warm bowls of phở and fresh herbs.

Fresh Herbs & Long-Simmered Broth

Vietnamese cuisine is defined by balance: sweet, salty, sour, and fresh. At Lang Van, we do not cut corners. Our signature beef phở broth is simmered with marrow bones, ginger, star anise, cloves, and cinnamon for 24 hours to lock in deep complexity.

We serve every phở bowl with a generous side plate of crisp bean sprouts, Thai basil, mint, sliced jalapeños, and fresh lime wedges, allowing each guest to customize their bowl to their exact preference.
