Private Dining Space

The Ember Room

The Ember Room offers a warm, design-forward setting for private dinners, social celebrations, and business gatherings. Connected to the energy of the main dining room while providing privacy for your group.

  • ✨ Long artisan walnut dining table & burgundy seating
  • 🕯️ Charcoal walls & copper candleholders
  • 🔥 View of central wood-fired hearth
  • 🍷 Dedicated sommelier & private service team
