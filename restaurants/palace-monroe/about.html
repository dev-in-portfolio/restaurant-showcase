Monroe Hospitality Since 1984

A beloved family-style diner and kitchen focused on high-quality ingredients, fair pricing, and unhurried service.

Diner Cooking Principles

At The Palace, we operate under classic family diner values. We hand-cut our steak fillets, bake pasta casserole fresh daily, and simmer our gravies using traditional recipes.

Our kitchen focuses on comforting textures, rich seasonings, and large Southern dinner sizes that satisfy every appetite.

Lancaster Avenue Landmark

Our dining room on Lancaster Avenue in Monroe NC serves as a local meeting spot. We maintain comfortable booth settings, a welcoming atmosphere, and friendly table service.

While we focus on relaxed dine-in experiences, we support fast counter takeout, pickup orders, and party pans catering for local Monroe events.
