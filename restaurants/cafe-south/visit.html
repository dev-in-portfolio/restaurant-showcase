Getting Here

Right off I-77 Exit 3.

Cafe South is located at the intersection of West Arrowood Road and Forest Pine Drive in South Charlotte. Take I-77 Exit 3 and head toward the intersection. You'll find the cafe near Whitehall and Ayrsley.

The restaurant is around the corner from Ramblewood Park. If you're heading to Carowinds, Cafe South is a natural stop just before or after the state line.
