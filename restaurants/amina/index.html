Welcome to Amina

Where Heritage Meets Fire

Born from the vision of Charlotte native and “food surgeon” Chef Chianti Peek, Amina is a celebration of Eritrean roots and Mediterranean craft. Every dish is shaped by heritage — from berbere-rubbed wings to wood-fired flatbreads — and every visit is an experience, whether it’s a weeknight dinner or a Saturday night in the lounge.

Our wood-fired program brings out the depth in everything from branzino to lamb chops. Our late-night lounge, with resident DJs spinning R&B and house, keeps the energy alive Friday and Saturday until 2 AM.
