Family Roots

Our Story

A family tradition of Vietnamese hospitality on South Boulevard since 1992.

Doan's Vietnamese Cuisine began with a simple vision: to share the vibrant, aromatic flavors of Saigon with Charlotte, NC.

Drawing on generations of traditional Vietnamese cooking techniques, the Doan family built a menu around the dishes they grew up eating: bone broth simmered slowly for 24 hours, spring rolls wrapped fresh to order, and broken rice plates.

Today, we continue to cook the way we always have — from scratch, with fresh ingredients, and without MSG. Every bowl of pho and plate of vermicelli is prepared with care and pride.

Key Pillars

• Established on South Boulevard
• 24-hour slow-simmered broths
• MSG-free scratch preparation
• Family owned and operated

Our Journey

1992
A New Beginning
The Doan family opens Doan's Vietnamese Cuisine on South Boulevard, serving authentic family recipes with eight tables.
2005
Growing Reputation
Word spreads about the slow-simmered bone broth phở. Regulars begin returning weekly, earning us a loyal local following.
2018
Expanding Choices
The kitchen expands to offer vermicelli bowls, lemongrass grilled combo plates, and fresh vegetarian options.
Today
Still Cooking with Love
Doan's remains a family operation — still MSG-free, still made from scratch, still welcoming everyone like family.
