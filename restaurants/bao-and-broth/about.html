Sourcing & Heritage

Our journey of bringing authentic steamed buns, slow-simmered broths, and fresh street food to Charlotte's Optimist Hall.

Chef Larry Schreiber

At Bao and Broth, our kitchen is guided by award-winning Chef Larry Schreiber. Chef Larry combines traditional street food culinary methods with modern culinary flair, crafting steamed buns, slow-cooked ramen, and fresh potstickers that highlight the bold, comforting flavors of Asian street stalls.

24-Hour Slow-Cooked Tonkotsu Broth

To create our signature Tonkotsu broth, pork bones are slow-simmered for a full 24 hours to maximize depth, richness, and body. We prepare our broth in small batches daily, ensuring that every bowl of ramen served at our counter maintains the high standard Chef Larry Schreiber is celebrated for.

Optimist Hall Culinary Stall

Located inside Charlotte's historic Optimist Hall food hall on N Brevard St, our counter stall serves walk-up guests, corporate catering packages, and online pick-up orders daily. We invite you to grab a warm bun, settle in with a steaming bowl of ramen, and experience Charlotte's premier street food destination.
