Hospitality You Can Feel

Orbit & Ember was created around a straightforward idea: thoughtful food and genuine hospitality should reinforce one another. From our visible wood-fired oven to the way our team guides guests through the menu, every detail is designed to feel considered without feeling formal.

"A restaurant earns trust in small moments—the welcome at the door, an honest recommendation, a carefully prepared plate, and the feeling that no one is rushing you out of your seat." — Mara Ellison, Founder & Owner

The Hearth

All of our dishes are grilled over cured oak firewood, delivering a deep, signature campfire finish to local beef, hand-tossed flatbreads, and fresh seasonal greens.

The Bar
