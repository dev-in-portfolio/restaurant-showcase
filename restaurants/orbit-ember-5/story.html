Leadership & Origin

Built Around The Table

Orbit & Ember was created by owner Mara Ellison and Executive Chef Julian Reyes around a shared belief: a restaurant can feel special without feeling formal.

The Origin Story

Orbit & Ember began with a simple idea: the most memorable meals are rarely only about the food. They are about the sound of a dining room settling into the evening, the first drink arriving at the table, the glow of an open wood-fired hearth, and the people who make guests feel that they have time to stay.

Founder Mara Ellison spent years working alongside independent hospitality businesses, fascinated by the difference between restaurants that merely served meals and those that created a genuine sense of belonging. She envisioned a restaurant that could feel special without feeling distant, creative without becoming unfamiliar.

Mara partnered with Executive Chef Julian Reyes, whose cooking philosophy was built around live fire, seasonal ingredients, and the belief that recognizable food becomes memorable when every detail is treated with care.

Restaurant Leadership

Mara Ellison

Founder & Owner

Mara leads Orbit & Ember's guest experience, brand direction, and long-term vision. She works closely with Chef Julian on menu development, dining room atmosphere, and the details that shape a guest's visit from arrival through the final course.

"A restaurant earns trust in small moments—the welcome at the door, an honest recommendation, a carefully prepared plate, and the feeling that no one is rushing you out of your seat."

Julian Reyes

Executive Chef

Executive Chef Julian Reyes leads the kitchen with a style grounded in live wood-fire cooking, seasonal ingredients, and contemporary American technique. Rather than disguising familiar foundations, he uses fire, smoke, acidity, and texture to deepen each flavor.

"Fire should add character to an ingredient, not cover it up. I want guests to recognize the dish before they discover what makes it different."

Restaurant Milestones

Our Journey in Charlotte's South End

2021

Concept Development

Mara Ellison begins developing the Orbit & Ember concept around live-fire cooking and approachable evening hospitality.

2022

Culinary Partnership

Mara partners with Chef Julian Reyes to shape the menu, open-kitchen hearth, and restaurant identity.

2023

Open Hearth Buildout

The team constructs the central blackened-steel wood-fired oven, walnut dining room, and copper cocktail lounge.

2024

Orbit & Ember Opens

Orbit & Ember opens in Charlotte as an independent contemporary American restaurant and cocktail bar.

2025

Brunch & Zero-Proof Expansion

Service expands to include wood-fired weekend brunch and an artisanal zero-proof cocktail program.

2026

Seasonal Program Refinement

A seasonal rhythm of menu updates is established while preserving signature guest favorites.
