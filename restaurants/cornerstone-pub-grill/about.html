Our Neighborhood Story

Connecting the Matthews community through great comfort food, rotating craft beer handles, and backyard games.

A Gathering Spot for Matthews

At Cornerstone, we set out to build a dependable neighborhood joint where you always know your bartender. We've become the default location to catch the game, meet up with coworkers after a long shift, or relax on the weekend with family. Our covered patio and open layout make it easy to host celebrations or just wind down.

Classic Tavern Cuisine

We focus on what a sports grill does best: serving food that hits the spot. Our kitchen prepares hand-pressed half-pound Angus burgers, house-made chipotle chicken breast sandwiches, and double-baked wings tossed in house sauces. We pair these favorites with a rotating line of local NC draft lagers and ales.

Game Day Heritage

Sports are in our blood. From local high school Friday night updates to weekend professional watch parties, we broadcast the action. With dedicated audio channels for major matchups and customized pitcher wing pack estimates, we ensure your squad is comfortable from kickoff to the final whistle.
