Tips for First-Timers

Make the most of your Balkan dining experience.

🥩

Order the Cevapi

If it's your first time, request the Veliki Ćevapi. It's the national dish of Bosnia for a reason.

☕

Linger Over Coffee

Traditional Bosnian coffee is prepared to be sipped slowly. Dino will remind you not to rush.

🛒

Browse the Market

You'll walk through the grocery aisles anyway. Don't miss out on taking home a box of regional sweets.

💳

Casual & Relaxed

Casual and family-friendly atmosphere. Cash and all major credit cards accepted.
