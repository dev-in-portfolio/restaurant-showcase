What We're Known For

Bosnian classics, fresh stone-baked bread, and a story of community.

Ćevapi

Skinless grilled beef sausages served inside our homemade fluffy lepinja bread with kajmak and ajvar. The national dish of Bosnia.

Our Story

From Srebrenica to Central Avenue.

In 1998, Dino Mehic and his wife Amela arrived in Charlotte as refugees of the Bosnian War, having lost Dino's father in the Srebrenica genocide. Building a new life, they established Bosna Market in 2003 to serve the gathering Balkan community on Central Avenue.

As the market grew, they stepped through the wall to open a cozy, adjacent dining room. Today, Euro Grill & Cafe stands as a neighborhood pillar of resilience and authentic home cooking.
