The Market Experience

Eat. Shop. Discover.

Before Euro Grill & Cafe became a dining room, it was Dino and Amela's neighborhood grocery store. Today, you still step past rows of tall European jars, dry cured meats, and imports before stepping down into our cozy diner room.

Our customers love to grab a plate of fresh hot cevaps, and then load up on specialty ingredients, ajvar spreads, and sodas to take home to their families. It is an authentic taste of Sarajevo right on Central Avenue.
