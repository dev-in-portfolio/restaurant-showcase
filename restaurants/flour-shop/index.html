Chef Trey Wilson's neighborhood gathering place in Myers Park.

At Flour Shop, we put the craft of pasta-making front and center. Our 24-seat U-shaped bar wraps directly around the open kitchen hearth, letting you watch flour and eggs transform into delicate linguine, pappardelle, and stuffed ravioli. Sourcing directly from Carolina growers and fishermen, we offer an intimate dining experience where every plate is cooked to order right before your eyes.

🍝

Handmade Daily

Every shape and texture is rolled and cut fresh from scratch each morning. Pull up a stool and watch the culinary craft up close.

🌿

Locally Gathered

Ingredients gathered from local Carolina farms and day-boat fishermen, plus seasonal highlights from our own kitchen gardens.

🔥

Wood-Fired Hearth

Our kitchen burns oak hardwood to roast meats and finish sauces, giving a rich, rustic depth to traditional Italian recipes.
