Walk-In Policy

We reserve a portion of our U-shaped counter seats for walk-ins every evening. If booking times are full, stop by the bar directly and we will accommodate you as quickly as possible. Enjoying drinks and snacks at the counter is the classic way to experience the open kitchen.

Creekside Dining

Patio seating is weather-permitting and managed on a first-come basis. Feel free to request the patio on your reservation notes, or speak to our host when arriving.

What to Expect

  • Hearth Cooking: Watch your pasta rolled, stuffed, and cooked in front of you.
  • 24 Stool Counter: The vibrant center of our restaurant surrounding the fire.
  • Myers Park Creek: A peaceful outdoor seating area among the trees.
  • Rotating Recipes: A fresh menu highlighting daily market arrivals.

Celebrations & Groups

Flour Shop is one of Myers Park's favorite spaces for anniversaries, date nights, and group gatherings. Share plates of burrata, pass around fresh pasta bowls, and toast over a curated bottle. For groups larger than six, please contact the restaurant directly.
