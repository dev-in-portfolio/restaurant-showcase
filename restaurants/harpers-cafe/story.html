Hospitality & Heritage

A neighborhood bistro serving the Pineville-Matthews community with comfort food for 35 years.

Bistro Roots

At Harper's Cafe, we represent the warmth of a neighborhood hub. We emphasize simple ingredients, home-baked sweets, and cozy spaces that make everyone feel welcome.

Our cafe is proud to serve families, local shopkeepers, and travelers in a clean, casual environment.

Park Place Legacy

Located at the Terrace at Park Place shopping center, we focus on scratch-made biscuits, seared sandwiches, pot pies, and locally curated coffee beans.

Whether you are stopping by for a morning pastry or picking up large catering orders for your company meeting, we guarantee the highest quality.
