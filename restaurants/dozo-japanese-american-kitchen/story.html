Our Journey

Our Story

From a local food truck to Charlotte's favorite intimate katsu counter.

DŌZO Japanese-American Kitchen started as a food truck — Katsu Kart Sando Shop — serving crispy panko katsu sandwiches at local breweries and pop-up events across Charlotte.

The response was immediate. Crowds formed, sandos sold out daily, and it became clear that Charlotte was hungry for bold, satisfying Japanese comfort food.

In 2022, the team opened their first brick-and-mortar on Thrift Road — an intimate 12-seat kitchen with a 6-seat chef's counter where guests could watch every cutlet get breaded and fried to order.

The name DŌZO means "please" or "go ahead" in Japanese — a warm invitation to eat well and enjoy. That spirit guides everything we do.
