Good Heart Philosophy

Our Story

Traditional Thai cooking and fresh sushi craftsmanship in Charlotte since 2009.

Deejai Thai has been a Charlotte favorite for over 17 years, serving authentic Thai cuisine and fresh sushi from its Providence Road location in Myers Park. Built on a foundation of traditional family recipes and the highest-quality ingredients, Deejai brings the bold, balanced flavors of Thailand to the Queen City.

The name "Deejai" means "good heart" in Thai — and that philosophy runs through everything we do. From the careful preparation of each curry paste to the artful rolling of every sushi maki, our team takes pride in creating food that nourishes both body and spirit.

We believe in spice that challenges, flavors that surprise, and service that makes you feel like family. Thank you for dining with us!
