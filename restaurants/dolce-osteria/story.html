Italian Tradition

Our Story

Bringing the authentic warmth and flavors of Florence to Charlotte's Dilworth neighborhood.

Dolce Osteria was born from a deep love for regional Italian cooking and the desire to create a welcoming neighborhood gathering place.

Every dish on our menu reflects time-honored techniques passed down through generations, from rolling out fresh egg noodles to slowly simmering wild boar ragù and making dessert pastries by hand.

We believe in the power of simplicity — importing select olive oils from Tuscany, sourcing local farm ingredients, and extruding our pasta shapes fresh every single morning.

Our Core Principles

• Freshly extruded pasta daily
• Imported cold-pressed oils
• Locally sourced organic greens
• Family owned and welcoming
