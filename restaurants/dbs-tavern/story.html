Our Story

Serving the Ballantyne community with pride since 1988.

DB's Tavern was founded by the Burgoon family — a dedicated team with deep roots in Charlotte's neighborhood restaurant scene. Already known for running favorite area sports spots, they saw a need for a welcoming neighborhood tavern in the growing Ballantyne area and decided to build one from the ground up.

The concept was simple: great food, cold beer, and a place where everyone knows your name. DB's quickly became exactly that — a go-to spot for families, sports fans, and neighbors looking for a reliable meal and a friendly face.

Over the years, DB's has become a Ballantyne institution. The walls are lined with TVs for game day, the kitchen stays open late, and the staff treats every guest like a regular. Whether you're coming in for lunch after a soccer game or closing the place down after a late shift, DB's is always ready for you.
