The Historic Parlor

Located inside a beautifully restored late-19th-century brick commercial storefront on Charles Street, The Portrait Gallery merges rustic historic brick walls with modern luxury finishes.

Our intimate parlor space acts as a living canvas where guests can relax under crystal chandeliers while sharing a conversation and exploring rotating art presentations.

Bespoke Flavor Mapping

We construct our culinary program around "Flavor Mapping"—focusing on plates that contrast rich fatty meats, smoky details, and acidic herbal garnishes.

Each cocktail is hand-shaken with house-pressed juices, wild syrups, and cold-pressed botanical infusions to ensure a complete sensory experience.
