The Beginning

Asian Corner Mall Staples

Starting inside Charlotte's Asian Corner Mall, Le's Sandwiches built its reputation as a beloved cash-only hidden gem serving the best hot baguettes in North Carolina.

Family Transition

Passing the Lemongrass Torch

After decades of hard work, founders Le Nguyen and Minh Nguyen passed the shop to their son, keeping the traditional recipes and warm community vibe intact for future generations.

Charlotte Staple

Toasted Perfection

We bake our own baguettes multiple times daily, ensuring every single Bánh Mì has the perfect crunchy shell and pillowy crumb that makes Vietnamese sandwiches famous.

Local Distinction

We use traditional spreads, house-made egg yolk mayonnaise, fresh cilantro, jalapeños, and pickled daikon/carrots for a perfect texture balance.

Community Focus

We are proud to serve Myers Park and Charlotte locals, carrying forward our tradition of authentic, affordable street-style sandwiches.
