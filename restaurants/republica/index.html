The Essence of Dominican Gastronomy

At República, we bring the vibrant colors, rich seasoning, and lively atmosphere of Santo Domingo straight to Matthews, NC. Our kitchen crafts gourmet versions of Dominican staples using traditional techniques, slow-simmered marinades, and fresh local ingredients.
