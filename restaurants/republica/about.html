Dominican Culinary Heritage

Matthews' premium destination for elevated Latin dining, craft mixology, and weekend lounge energy.

Gourmet Dominican Gastronomy

At República, we focus on elevated interpretations of traditional Dominican recipes. We select high-quality pork belly for our chicharrón and double-fry our green plantains for crispy tostones.

Our sauces—from spicy habanero relishes to rich coconut reductions—are cooked from scratch daily to capture the rich tropical profile of the Caribbean.

Lounge & Cocktails

We combine a comfortable family-friendly dining room with a stylish evening cocktail lounge. Guests can sit at the main bar counter to enjoy tropical craft cocktail mixes.

We offer late-night hours on weekends with live music, making República Matthews' favorite spot for celebrations and group events.
