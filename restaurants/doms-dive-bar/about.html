Our Story

About Dom's

South Blvd's neighborhood dive — unfussy, affordable, and yours.

Dom's Dive Bar is exactly what it sounds like — a comfy neighborhood sports pub where the pints are cheap, the food is craveable, and nobody pretends to be anything they're not.

Tucked into a strip on South Boulevard in Sedgefield, Dom's is the kind of place where you walk in, grab a pool cue, and suddenly it's last call.

With two free slate pool tables, dart lanes, board games, and an impressive TV system, we are built for hanging out with neighbors.
