📋 Reservation Policies

  • Grace Period: Reserved tables are held for 15 minutes after the reservation time. Please call if your party is running late.
  • Booking Window: Reservations open up to 30 days in advance daily at 10:00 AM.
  • Complete Party Seating: We seat parties once all guests have arrived so the table can begin service together.
  • Seating Requests: Patio and specific table requests are welcomed but subject to weather and availability.

🚶 Walk-In Hospitality

Walk-in guests are welcomed whenever space is available. A portion of our walnut bar and lounge is reserved nightly for walk-in dining.

During peak weekend hours, our team will quote an estimated wait or add your party to the live waitlist.
